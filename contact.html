Frequently Asked Questions

I purchased MedPYQ Pro, but my account still says "Free Plan".

Sometimes it takes a moment for the database to sync. Please try logging out of your account and logging back in. If the issue persists, email us at medpyq@gmail.com with your Razorpay Payment ID and registered email address.

Can I use my MedPYQ account on multiple devices?

For security reasons and to prevent account sharing, MedPYQ supports active login on only one device at a time. If you log in on a second device, you will be automatically logged out of the first one.

I found an error in a question's explanation. How do I report it?

We strive for 100% accuracy. If you spot a mistake, please email us with a screenshot of the question and the correct reference. Our medical team will review and update it immediately.
